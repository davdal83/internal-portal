About Papa John's Jacksonville

At Papa John's Jacksonville, we’re more than just your neighborhood pizza place — we’re a passionate team dedicated to serving the vibrant communities of Jacksonville, St Augustine, and Yulee, Florida.

From the first dough stretch to the last savory slice, we focus on quality and care in every pizza we make. Using only the freshest ingredients, our commitment is to bring you bold flavors and that perfect crispy crust you love.

We’re proud to be part of these amazing cities — where family, fun, and community spirit are at the heart of everything. Whether it’s a game night, a celebration, or just a craving for great pizza, we’re here to deliver warmth and smiles straight to your door.

Join us as we continue to grow and give back, supporting local events, teams, and neighbors. At Papa John's Jacksonville, it’s not just pizza — it’s a slice of home.
